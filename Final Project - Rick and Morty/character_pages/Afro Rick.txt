'''{{PAGENAME}}''' is an alternate version of [[Rick Sanchez]] with an afro, and of African-American descent.

==Biography==
He first appeared in "[[The Rickshank Rickdemption]]", in [[The Citadel]] talking to other Ricks while [[Morty]] and [[Summer]] were escorted past them to the [[Council of Ricks]]. He was talking to [[Alien Rick]] and an unnamed Rick.
[[File:Black and Alien Rick.png|thumb|{{PAGENAME}} with black hair in ''The Rickshank Rickdemption'']]
He is later seen in "[[Tales From the Citadel]]", firstly appearing backstage during the presidential debate, after [[Juggling Rick]] is seen on TV. He is in a heated discussion with [[Cowboy Rick]]. He is later seen in the crowd of Ricks at the time of [[President Morty|President Morty's]] attempted assassination and is greatly upset following the shooting.
