}}'''Alien Morty''' is an alternate version of [[Morty Smith]] who appeared in the episode [[Close Rick-Counters of the Rick Kind]].

==Appearance==
Alien Morty is an alien teenage boy. He has slimy, lime-green skin. He has two curly <span>antennae</span> on his head and short brown hair. He has three slightly squinty eyes, with one of them on his forehead and he also has a big nose. He has pointed elf-like ears, He wears a yellow shirt and blue pants, and has 4 arms.
