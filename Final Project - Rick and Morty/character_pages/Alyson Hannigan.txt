'''Alyson Hannigan''' was an alien public defender who appeared in the episode [[Amortycan Grickfitti]].

== History ==
Alyson was assigned to defend [[Morty Smith|Morty]], [[Summer Smith|Summer]], and [[Bruce Chutback]] after they were accused of reckless driving and manslaughter. However, [[Space Cruiser|Rick's ship]] killed her before she arrived and reanimated her body via a control chip. Rick's car urged Morty and Summer to let Bruce take the fall for the crimes, but when they refused the car detonated the chip in Alyson's head, allowing the car to rescue them from the prison.

== Trivia ==
* Her name is a reference to actress Alyson Hannigan.
