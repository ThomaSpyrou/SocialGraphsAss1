[[File:Arcade_Alien_Guy.png|260px|thumb]]The '''Arcade Alien''' is a minor character who made his debut in [[Mortynight Run]]. His role in that episode consisted of him reacting in a surprising way to a high score run of [[Roy: A Life Well Lived]] played by [[Rick Sanchez]] in [[Blips and Chitz]]. He made a couple of appearances in later episodes of Season 2 and Season 3 as a background character.
==Appearance==
The Arcade Alien is an alien who mainly has red skin with some of the skin appearing to be more pinkish on his face and belly. He has like ears that look like small bat wings and has a reptilian-like tail. He also wears a yellow T-shirt with a cat-like design on it.
==Personality==
Arcade Alien is loud, obnoxious, and over-excited about everything.
