'''Calypso '''was a sorceress and former member of [[The Vindicators]], who died on their second adventure, along with [[Lady Katana]] and [[Diablo Verde]].

==Appearance==
[[File:Rick's_ride.png|thumb|Calypso appearing in the ride created by drunk Rick]]
She was never seen, but she was mentioned in "[[Vindicators 3: The Return of Worldender]]". She dresses as a shaman with a large feathered headdress, facepaint, and gold jewelry, and wields a magic staff. Calypso can apparently summon blue flames from her hands, but the full extent of her powers is unknown.

==Trivia==
*The name Calypso refers to a style of Afro-Caribbean music that originated from African slaves working on sugar plantations in the Caribbean and Cuba.
*She is a parody of [[Wikipedia:Enchantress (DC Comics)|Enchantress]] from ''[[Wikipedia:Suicide Squad (film)|Suicide Squad]]''.
