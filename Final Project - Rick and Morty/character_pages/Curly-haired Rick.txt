'''Curly-haired Rick''' is an alternate version of [[Rick Sanchez]] that appeared in the episode [[The Rickshank Rickdemption]]. He is a rick with curly hair, a mustache and a beard.

In same episode, many Ricks are killed when the [[Citadel of Ricks]] is transported into a prison of the [[Galactic Federation]], it is unknown if Curly-haired Rick is one of the Ricks who died.

==Appearance==
Curly-haired Rick is a tall old man, with grey curly hair, a unibrow, a mustache and a beard. He wears a white lab coat over a blue shirt and brown pants with black shoes.
