'''Dangelians''' are octopus-like aliens that exist in the world of ''[[Rick and Morty]]''. One can be seen in the [[Galactic Federation]] Prison when [[Rick]] is put in maximum security.

==Appearance==
Each of their three tentacles has an individual eye at the end. They have normal four fingered hands and legs, and are of a purple color. They do not appear to have any form of mouth in their face. They are quite similar to [[Kozbians]].
