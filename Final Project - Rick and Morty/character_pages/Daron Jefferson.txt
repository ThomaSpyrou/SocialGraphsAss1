}}'''Daron Jefferson''' is an alien from the episode [[Auto Erotic Assimilation]], that is under the control of [[Unity]]. When he was released of his slavery to Unity, it was revealed that he used to be a landscaper back when he had free will. it was also revealed that he was a racist, who believed that the cone-nippled aliens were superior to the ring-nippled aliens. He helped start a race war between his own kind and the ring-nippled aliens.
