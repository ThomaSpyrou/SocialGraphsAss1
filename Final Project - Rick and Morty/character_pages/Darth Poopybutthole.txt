==Biography==
In "Never Ricking Morty", Darth Poopybutthole is a member of the army facing off against [[Rick]] and [[Morty]], alongside [[Evil Morty]], the [[Citadel's militia]], a large group of [[Mr. Meeseeks]], and an army of male [[Gazorpians]].

==Appearance==
Darth Poopybutthole is an evil version of [[Mr. Poopybutthole]], modeled after Emperor Palpatine (also known as Darth Sidious) from ''Star Wars''. He has red eyes, ashen grey skin with large vein-like markings, and a tattered black cloak.

==Relationships==
He is seen with [[Evil Morty]] and his army, presumably an important figure in the hierarchy.

He ends the episode proclaiming his newfound faith to [[Jesus Christ]].
