'''Diablo Verde''' was a Mexican superhero and a member of [[The Vindicators]] who was mentioned in "[[Vindicators 3: The Return of Worldender]]". He was never seen, but he was mentioned to have died, along with [[Lady Katana]] and [[Calypso]], in battle with [[Doom-Nomitron]].

==Appearance==
[[File:Rick's_ride.png|thumb|Diablo Verde appearing in the ride created by drunk Rick]]
Although Diablo Verde never personally appears in the episode, an image of him could be seen in the mobile tablet Morty was holding in his bed. He appears as a large, muscular horned demon with green skin, a giant handlebar mustache, and several tattoos that resemble Maya or Inca iconography.

==Trivia==
*His name means "Green Devil" in Spanish.
*He is a parody of [[Wikipedia:El Diablo (comics)|El Diablo]] from ''[[Wikipedia:Suicide Squad (film)|Suicide Squad]]'' and [[Wikipedia:Hulk (comics)|Hulk]] from [[Wikipedia:Marvel Comics|Marvel Comics]].
* His tattoos resemble those of [[Wikipedia:Drax the Destroyer|Drax]] from ''[[Wikipedia:Guardians of the Galaxy (film)|Guardians of the Galaxy]]''.
* He also appears to be a parody of [[Wikipedia:Hellboy|Hellboy]] and [[Wikipedia:Blue Devil|Blue Devil]].
