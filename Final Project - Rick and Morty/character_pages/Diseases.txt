'''Diseases''' are characters that are featured in the episode ''[[Anatomy Park (episode)|Anatomy Park]]''. They are found inside the attraction also named [[Anatomy Park (Location)|Anatomy Park]], inside the homeless man [[Ruben]].

{{-}}
==Tuberculosis==
[[File:Tuberculosis.jpg|thumb|260px]]'''[[Wikipedia:Tuberculosis|Tuberculosis]]''' is a lung disease. They are portrayed as monstrous, spider beings, living in [[Ruben|Ruben's]] lungs.

The tuberculosis infecting Ruben were dormant until sabotaged by [[Poncho]].

{{-}}
==Gonorrhea==
[[File:Gonorrhea.jpg|thumb|260px]]
'''[[Wikipedia:Gonorrhea|Gonorrhea]]''' resides in [[Ruben|Ruben's]] intestines. It confronted the gang while they were in the small intestine and almost killed them, but was blown up by [[Poncho]] when a lit match was thrown into the gas clouds of Ruben's GI tract.Â 

{{-}}
==Hepatitis A==
[[File:Hepatitis_A.jpg|thumb|260px]]'''[[Wikipedia:Hepatitis A|Hepatitis A]]''' is a giant, monstrous being based on the monster from the movie [[Wikipedia:Cloverfield|Cloverfield]], living inside of [[Ruben]]. Hepatitis A was infecting Ruben's liver until it came across [[Morty]] and the gang. It wasn't until the very end of the adventure when Morty and [[Annie]] had their final encounter with Hepatitis A, when he was just about to kill them, only to be stopped by Hepatitis C.

{{-}}
==Hepatitis C==
[[File:Hepatitis_C.jpg|thumb|260px]]
When [[Morty]] and [[Annie]] were being chased by Hepatitis A, '''[[Wikipedia:Hepatitis C|Hepatitis C]]''' appears and grabs Hepatitis A with its jaws, saving their lives. Hepatitis C gives them a thumbs up before walking away, carrying Hepatitis A away in its mouth.

{{-}}
==Bubonic Plague==
[[File:Bubonic_Plague.jpg|thumb]]'''[[Wikipedia:Bubonic Plague|Bubonic Plague]]''' (also known as '''Black Death''') is a rare but serious bacterial infection that's transmitted by fleas. [[Poncho]] attempted to smuggle some of the plague out of [[Ruben]] to sell to the highest bidder. Bubonic Plague bites Poncho on the shoulder, causing him to stumble off a high balcony to his death.

{{-}}

==E. Coli==
[[File:E_Coli.jpg|thumb]]
'''E. coli''' is a portrayal of [[Wikipedia:Escherichia coli|E. coli]], a bacterium that is commonly found in the lower intestine of warm-blooded organisms. A swarm of E. Coli attacked and killed [[Dr. Xenon Bloom]] on a railway station, as well as [[Morty Smith|Morty]] and [[Annie]], who managed to escape most of them by train, but some of them managed to get on the train. This version of E. coli appears to be a [[wikipedia:bacteriophage|bacteriophage]] as opposed to an actual bacterium.
