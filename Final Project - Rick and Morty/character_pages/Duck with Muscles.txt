'''Duck with Muscles''' is an unnamed false character from the episode "[[Total Rickall]]".

==Biography==
He was a larger [[Alien Parasites|parasite]] who, with [[Baby Wizard]] and [[Morty Smith]], escorted [[Rick Sanchez]] to the Garage to be killed, as Morty didn't want to do it in front of [[Pencilvester]].

Duck with Muscles was shot and killed by Morty shortly after Morty shot and killed Baby Wizard. Before dying, he said "Oh, wow...Baby Wizard was a Parasite? He set me up with my wif-" before getting shot.

==Appearance==
Duck with Muscles is a giant, yellow duck with beady black eyes and an orange beak and orange webbed feet. He also has giant, muscular human arms with anchors tattooed on them.
