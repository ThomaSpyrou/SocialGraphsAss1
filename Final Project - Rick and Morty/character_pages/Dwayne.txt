== Biography ==
The original Dwayne presumably became a Cronenberg in the [[Cronenberged dimension]].

Dwayne is the vice president of the United States and makes his first appearance in [[Rick & Morty's Thanksploitation Spectacular|Rick & Morty's Thanksploitation Spectactular]]. The President mentions that Dwayne is only vice president because the President needs 10% of the white vote.
