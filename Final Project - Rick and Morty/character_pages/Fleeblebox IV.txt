'''Fleeblebox IV''' is a news anchorman at [[Glub 7 News]] station. He is from a planet where every creature has three eyes.

==Appearance==
Fleeblebox is a middle-aged blue three-eyed alien with an odd nose, long ears and tentacle arms with yellow suctioncups. He has grey hair and a few wrinkles in his face.
