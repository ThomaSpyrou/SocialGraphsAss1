'''Glarb''' is a field reporter for [[Glub 7 News]]. She covered the arrest of [[Rick Sanchez (C-132)|Rick (C-132)]] and [[Morty Smith (C-132)|Morty (C-132)]] after they had a twelve-hour standoff with the Time Police. She was unable to get a comment from [[Professor Tock|Detective Tock]] or [[Jerry Smith (C-132)|Jerry (C-132)]]

==Appearance==
Glarb is a green, three-eyed alien with an upturned nose, long ears, protrusions on her neck, and tentacles for arms. She has grey hair and a few wrinkles on her face, giving her a middle-aged appearance.
