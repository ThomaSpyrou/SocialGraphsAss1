==Appearance==
The ideal Jerry in [[Beth Smith|Beth]]'s eyes, is a taller, selfless, more muscular version of him with a thick belt buckle around his waist.

Each one is still significantly weaker than [[Beth's Mytholog]], although a half dozen of them can hold her down temporarily.
