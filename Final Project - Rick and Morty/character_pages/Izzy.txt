}}'''Izzy''' is the cat of one of the [[Smith Family]]'s neighbors. She appeared in "[[Pickle Rick]]".
==Biography==
After [[Rick Sanchez]], in the form of a [[Pickle Rick (character)|pickle]] was left abandoned by his daughter and grandchildren in the garage, Izzy walked in and mistook him for a snake. She pawed at Rick and knocked him off the table, making him roll out into the driveway, where he suffered from the blazing sun.
