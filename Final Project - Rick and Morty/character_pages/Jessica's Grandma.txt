}}'''Jessica's Grandma''' is the deceased, unnamed grandmother of [[Jessica]]. She appeared via picture of her corpse in the episode "[[Edge of Tomorty: Rick Die Rickpeat]]".
==Biography==
Jessia's Grandma died sometime in 2019 and had an open casket funeral. In her will, she left her necklace to her granddaughter, [[Jessica]]. Jessica happily posted a picture of herself, wearing this necklace on Instagram, with her dead grandmother's corpse, lying in the background.
==Trivia==
*She is currently the only other known family member of [[Jessica|Jessica's]] family, aside of Jessica, herself.
