== Biography ==
The original Kathy Ireland presumably became a Cronenberg in the [[Cronenberged dimension]].

Kathy Ireland is [[Blazen|Blazen's]] wife and a swimsuit model for Sports Illustrated. She asks [[Morty Smith|Morty]] if Blazen was wearing her thong but she states that Blazen probably told Morty not to tell her. However, she says she hopes Blazen was wearing her thong because even though he was ashamed of it, people should always be honest. Morty tells her that Blazen was wearing her thong to which Kathy Ireland calls him a "gross animal pig."
