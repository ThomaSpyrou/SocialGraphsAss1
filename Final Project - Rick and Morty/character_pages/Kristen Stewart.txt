==Biography==
Kristen Stewart went on Leonardo DiCaprio's yacht with the Jerry Smith in her dimension, who was a famous movie star. Kristen proceeded to have sex with Jerry while on the boat, which [[Jerry Smith]] witnessed through the [[Interdimensional Goggles]]. Their time together on the boat happened shortly before Jerry's big release party for his new movie, "[[Last Will and Testimeow: Weekend at Dead Cat Lady's House II]]", at which he later suffered a nervous breakdown.
