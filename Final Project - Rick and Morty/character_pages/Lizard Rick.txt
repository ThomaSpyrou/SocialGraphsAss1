'''Lizard Rick''' is a lizard person-version of [[Rick]] seen climbing walls at The Citadel.

==Biography==
He first appeared in the [[Season 3]] premiere episode, "[[The Rickshank Rickdemption]]", and can be spotted crawling on the walls of the [[Citadel of Ricks|Citadel]]. It is unknown what happened to him following the fall of the [[Galactic Federation]] and near destruction of [[The Citadel]]. 

He most likely has affiliations with [[Lizard Morty|lizard morty]].
