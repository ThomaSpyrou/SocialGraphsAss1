}}'''Long Sleeved Morty''' is an alternate version of [[Morty Smith]] who appeared in the episode [[Close Rick-Counters of the Rick Kind]].

In [[The Rickshank Rickdemption]], many Mortys are killed when the [[Citadel of Ricks]] is transported into a prison of the [[Galactic Federation]], it is unknown if Long Sleeved Morty is one of the Mortys who died.

==Appearance==
Long Sleeved Morty is a young Caucasian teenage boy with short, curly brown hair, and a yellow long-sleeved shirt or sweater that only shows his hands. He also wears blue pants.
