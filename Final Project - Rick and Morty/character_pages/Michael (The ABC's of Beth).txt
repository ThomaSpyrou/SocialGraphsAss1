'''Michael '''is a character that has a voice only appearance in the episode [[The ABC's of Beth]]

==Biography==
Michael works at an Antique Phone Rentals store that rented Jerry an answering machine. Although Michael does not make a full appearance he calls [[Jerry Smith|Jerry]] saying that no one really needs the answering machine.
