'''Michael Denny and the Denny Singers''' is a group of people who paid for an ad urging people to vote no on [[Proposition XW2]], a ballot measure that would allow "gay...errr [[Trunk People]] to get married". The group is first mentioned in the episode "[[Rixty Minutes]]".

Michael Denny also bears a strong resemblance to Donald Trump.
