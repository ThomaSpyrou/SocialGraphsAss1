}}'''Piece of Toast''' is a famous actor from an unspecified dimension.

Piece of Toast has made his career with appearances on that dimension's ''[[Saturday Night Live (Inter-Dimensional Cable)|Saturday Night Live]]'' and has broken into film industry taking on more serious roles. According to [[Rick]], Piece of Toast and [[Bobby Moynihan]] hate each other and have "creative differences".

In "[[The Ricks Must Be Crazy]]," Piece of Toast is shown on a poster for his feature film ''French Toast, ''hanging on the outside of [[Egan Cinema]].
