'''Plumber Rick''' is an alternate version of [[Rick Sanchez]] who first appeared in the episode, "[[Tales From the Citadel]]." He is a plumber on who lives on [[The Citadel]].

== Appearance ==
Plumber Rick shares the appearance of [[Rick Sanchez|Rick C-137]] but wears a backward ball cap, has a blue mustache, and wears overalls instead of a lab coat.
