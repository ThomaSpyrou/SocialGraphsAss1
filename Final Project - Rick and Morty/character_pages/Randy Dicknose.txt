'''Randy Dicknose''' is a host on the new hit show MAN vs. CAR. He is seen on a television programme from one of infinite dimensions.

== Biography ==
He hosts the MAN vs. CAR show. His co-host [[Michael McLick]] remains quiet through the segment, leaving him to carry on with the segment by himself. As the car defeats the man, he starts laughing, stating how the cars always win.
