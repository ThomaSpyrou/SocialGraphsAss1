{{Character|name = Reverse Rick Outrage|species = Human|age = 70|job = Candidate|first = Tales From the Citadel|status = Deceased|image = ReverseRickOutRage.PNG|affiliation = The [[Citadel of Ricks]]}}'''Reverse Rick Outrage''' was a presidential candidate in the [[The Citadel|Citadel]] Election in "[[Tales From the Citadel]]." His character is likely designed to resemble U.S. Senator and former presidential candidate Bernie Sanders.

== Appearance ==
Reverse Rick Outrage is a Rick that looks like the U.S.A's Senator and former candidate [[wikipedia:Bernie Sanders|Bernie Sanders]].

== Biography ==
Reverse Rick Outrage was one of the runners up for the President of [[The Citadel]], he failing, like the other candidates and [[Adjudicator Rick|The Adjudicator]], to take [[President Morty]] seriously in the campaign.

At some point, during the episode, he is killed on the orders of [[Evil Morty]], with his lifeless body later being seen floating in space with other Ricks and Mortys ejected from the Citadel.
