{{Character|name = Rick Guilt Rick|species = Human|age = 70|status = Unknown (Presumed deceased)|job = Candidate|affiliation = Citadel of Ricks|first = Tales From the Citadel|image = S3e7 rick guilt rick.png|voice actor=[[Justin Roiland]]}}'''Rick Guilt Rick''' was a presidential candidate in the [[Citadel of Ricks|Citadel]] Election in "[[Tales From the Citadel]]." His character is designed to resemble [[wikipedia:Jimmy McMillan|Jimmy McMillan]], a politician affiliated with the "Rent is Too Damn High Party," a populist political organization based in New York state.

He can be seen in a side room, along with [[Retired General Rick]], during the scene where the bodies of Ricks killed by Evil Morty's order are being loaded up in the airlocks to be disposed into space.

It's unknown if he was present and died during the destruction of the Citadel or left it and survived his likely demise.
