|image=Robot_Morty.png|species = Robot|family = [[Robot Rick]] (grandfather)}}'''Robot Morty''' is an alternate version of [[Morty Smith]] who appeared in the episode [[Close Rick-Counters of the Rick Kind]]. He is a robotic version of Morty who is dressed up to look like him.
==Appearance==
Robot Morty is a gray metal robot who is built in the body form of a teenage boy. He has brown hair and a yellow shirt, which are both non-robotic for some reason. He has robotic claw hands and red glowing pupils along with a giant mouth.
