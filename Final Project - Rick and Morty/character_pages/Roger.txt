'''Roger''' was a member of the team working on [[Anatomy Park (Location)|Anatomy Park]]. He appears in the episode of the same name ''[[Anatomy Park (Episode)|Anatomy Park]]''.

==Background==

Roger appeared to be the "zoo keeper" of [[Dr. Xenon Bloom|Dr. Xenon Bloom's]] team at Anatomy Park. He was killed when the Sphincter Dam blows.

== Trivia ==
* The appearance of Roger most likely comes from that of [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Steve_Irwin Steve Irwin] - an Australian zookeeper and wildlife expert.
