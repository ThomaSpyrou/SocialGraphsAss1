The '''Shlaammi''' is a species of alien creatures from another dimension. It appeared in the episode "[[Interdimensional Cable 2: Tempting Fate]]". Little is known about their species, except for the fact that one of them works in a factory for making [[Plumbus]]es. Their job consists of rubbing the dinglebop and then spitting on it, after it has been pushed through the grumbo.
