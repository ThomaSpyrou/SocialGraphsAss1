'''Severnians'''<ref>[[Rick and Morty Character Guide]] page 81</ref> are an alien race that appears in the world of Rick and Morty. They have appeared in the background of many episodes.

==Appearance==
Severnians are a cross between insect and mammal species. They are bipedal and have two arms. Their face does not have any eyes and it only consists of a mouth that is surrounded by four very huge fang like tusks and their mouth is always open. They have blue skin.
