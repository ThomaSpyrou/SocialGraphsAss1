'''Shimshamians''' are three-eyed creatures that exist in the universe of [[Rick and Morty]]. They can sometimes be seen in the background among other aliens.



==Appearance==
Shimshamians are tall humanoid aliens seen with yellow or purple skin. They have three eyes sticking up from the top of their head, their mouth stretches across their whole face with thick lips, three pairs of nostrils are on a row in the middle of their face and one ear on each side of their head. They possess two legs and two arms with four fingers on each hand. They have circular patterns on their arms.
