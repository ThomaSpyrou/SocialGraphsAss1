'''Shmlona Shmlobinson''' is a parody on Mona Robinson from ''[[Wikipedia:Who's the Boss?|Who's the Boss?]]''. She appeared in the episode "[[Rixty Minutes]]". She is an interdimensional being who comes from a dimension where every noun begins with the "Shml" prefix. She is on a show called ''[[Shmlo's the Shmloss?]]''.
