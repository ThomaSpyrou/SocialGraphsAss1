'''Shmlony Shmlicelli''' is a character who appeared in the episode "[[Rixty Minutes]]". He is a character from the series ''[[Shmlo's the Shmloss?]]'', an alternate version of ''[[Wikipedia:Who's the Boss?|Who's the Boss?]]''. He is a parody on Tony Micelli. He comes from an alternate dimension where all nouns have been replaces with the "Shml" prefix.
