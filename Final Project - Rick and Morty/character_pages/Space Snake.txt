'''Space Snake''' is a '''Rare''' Harvesting Tool in Fortnite: Battle Royale, that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 800 V-Bucks or with the Mecha Morty Bundle for 2,200 V-Bucks. It was first released in Chapter 2: Season 7 and is part of the Morty, C-137 Set.
