[[File:Garage.png|260px|thumb|The mini-laboratory table.]]'''The Garage''' serves as the primary workshop, and occasional living quarters of [[Rick Sanchez]]. There is a large, well-hidden laboratory underneath. It has an AI, similar to the [[Space Cruiser]].

== Secret Areas ==
In "[[Total Rickall]]", it is revealed that if you turn the knob of the washing machine, it opens up to a secret compartment filled with guns.

In "[[The Whirly Dirly Conspiracy]]", while [[Summer Smith|Summer]] is looks for the [[Morphizer-Xe]], she put the pins on the bulletin board in a position revealing a small compartment filled with sci-fi stuff. The stuff have unknown purposes.

== Appearance ==

The door near the shelves leads to the [[Kitchen]].
