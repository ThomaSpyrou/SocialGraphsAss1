[[File:S1e10 screaming mortys.png|thumb|260px]]The '''Tortured Mortys''' are a large number of Mortys that were being held in [[Evil Rick|Evil Rick's]] torture chamber. They appeared in [[Close Rick-Counters of the Rick Kind]]. When [[Morty Smith]] rescued [[Rick]], he pressed a button releasing the Tortured Mortys from their confines. At the end of the episode, they were shown alongside the other Mortys being sent back to their original homes.
