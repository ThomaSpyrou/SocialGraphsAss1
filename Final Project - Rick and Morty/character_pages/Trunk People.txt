'''Trunk People''' are a synthetic species of human-like creatures that have trunks on their faces. Scientists gave them trunks that allows them to have sex with both male and female partners.

==History==
They are first mentioned in the episode "[[Rixty Minutes]]" through two commercials regarding a ballot measure called [[Proposition XW2]] that would allow Trunk People to get married.

A [[Trunk Morty|trunk person]] [[Morty Smith|Morty]] is seen being treated at a hospital in the [[Citadel of Ricks]] in "[[Tales From the Citadel]]."
