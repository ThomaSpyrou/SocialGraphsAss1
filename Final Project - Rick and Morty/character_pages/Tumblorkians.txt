The '''Tumblorkians''' are a blobby, four-eyed slime alien race that exist in the world of Rick and Morty. According to [[Shrimply Pibbles]] the Tumblorkians have acid for blood. At one point a huge group of Tumblorkians held a protest with Shrimply Pibbles. They are known to inhabit planet Plopstar.<ref>https://prison.galacticfederation.com/prisoners/PL-5925-I</ref>
