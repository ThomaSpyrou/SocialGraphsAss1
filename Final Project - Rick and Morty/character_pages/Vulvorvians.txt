}}'''Vulvorvians''' are strange non-humanoid creatures inhabiting parts of the universe. 

==Appearance==
They have purple skin, while their hair and their ends of their appendages are yellow. They have a vertical mouth and many blue eyes. They do not wear clothes. 

==History==
A Vulvorian was quick to travel to [[Earth]] once it became the newest member of the [[Galactic Federation]]. Another Vulvorian was held in [[Galactic Federation Prison]] at the same time as [[Rick Sanchez]], and most likely escaped during the events of [[The Citadel]]'s teleportation into the prison.
